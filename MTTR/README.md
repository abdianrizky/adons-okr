- **October-November Volatility:** Resolution times peaked at 30+ hours in late October and November
- **Response Time Spikes:** November 10 showed 26.55 hours response time (outlier event)
- **Resolution Variability:** Wide range from 3.02 to 66.58 hours suggests inconsistent task complexity

### Recommendations

1. **Investigate Q4 Complexity Drivers**

   - Determine if external dependencies, scope creep, or resource constraints caused delays

2. **Implement Task Triage System**
   - Categorize tasks by complexity (Simple/Medium/Complex)
   - Set differentiated SLA targets based on complexity tiers

3. **Maintain December's Best Practices**
   - Document what contributed to December's excellent response times (0.55-2.5 hours)
   - Replicate these practices in future sprints

4. **Scale High-Volume Week Strategies**
   - Sept 1 and Nov 24 show ability to handle 12-14 tasks with high compliance
   - Formalize the approach used during these successful high-volume periods

5. **Address Outlier Events**
   - Nov 10 (66.58h resolution, 26.55h response) needs root cause analysis
